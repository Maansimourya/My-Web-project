Because every cake has a story to tell.

One of the most common events where cake is served is at birthdays. A birthday cake is often the centerpiece of the birthday party and is enjoyed by all of the guests. It is a tradition that dates back to ancient Rome, where cakes were made with honey and fruit and served at celebrations. Cake is also a common sight at weddings, where it is often served as dessert. Wedding cakes can be elaborate and ornate, with multiple tiers and intricate decorations. They are often a reflection of the couple's personalities and can be customized to match the theme of the wedding..
